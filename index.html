Thank you for your interest in the survey - 'The State of AI Adoption in Drug-Developing Biotech Companies'.

Introduction

The survey is part of a research project conducted at Goethe Business School, Frankfurt, contributing to a master's thesis in Pharmaceutical Business Administration. It aims to understand the current state and future plans of AI adoption within biotech companies actively engaged in drug discovery and development. Your insights are crucial in shaping an accurate picture of the industry's adoption of AI technologies, informing future trends and strategic recommendations.

Purpose

The goal of this research is to identify trends, challenges, and opportunities in AI implementation across the biotech sector. Your participation contributes to a comprehensive analysis that will highlight key areas for innovation and improvement, benefiting both academic and industry practices.

Target Group

This survey specifically targets professionals at biotech companies that are directly involved in drug discovery or development processes. Your expertise and experience are vital to the depth and relevance of the findings.

Time Commitment

The survey is designed to take only 3-5 minutes of your time.

Confidentiality and Data Privacy

Please be assured that your survey responses are processed anonymously. If you opt to provide your email address or company name, these will be stored separately and securely, ensuring that your responses cannot be linked back to your identity. While naming your company is entirely voluntary, it helps enhance the diversity of the survey responses and increases the quality of the research. Any such information will be treated with the highest confidentiality, stored on secure, GDPR-compliant servers until the survey's conclusion in August 2024, and will not be associated with your survey responses in any analysis or report.

Consent for Data Processing:

You are being asked to provide specific personal data (optionally email address and company name) as part of this research survey. Providing this information is entirely voluntary. By entering your data, you are giving explicit consent to processessing of this information only for the purposes detailed below. The personal data collected will be used exclusively for:

  • Sending a summary of the survey results, if requested.
  • Contacting you for a possible follow-up interview to deepen the insights obtained from the survey, if offered.
  • Enhancing the diversity of the survey responses, thereby improving the research quality.

Your data will be stored on secure, GDPR-compliant servers and will only be accessible by me. Robust security measures are implemented to ensure the confidentiality and integrity of your data.

Your Rights Under GDPR:

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have several rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: You have the right to request copies of your personal data.
  • Right to Rectification: You can request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate or complete information you believe is incomplete.
  • Right to Erasure: You can request that we erase your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You have the right to request that we restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • Right to Object to Processing: You have the right to object to our processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • Right to Data Portability: You have the right to request that we transfer the data that we have collected to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.
